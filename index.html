About

Two disciplines,
one person.

I run operations for a SaaS company in Texas, remotely from Lagos. I also build web products on the side. Those two things are less separate than they sound.

The same instinct that makes me redesign a broken support workflow makes me build a WhatsApp inquiry cart into a jewelry brand's website. I think in systems. I ship things that work.

My technical background is self-taught: HTML, CSS, JS, React, Node.js, Prisma, PostgreSQL, Paystack. I also build with AI — designing multi-step workflows, writing structured prompts, and integrating models like Claude, Gemini, and GPT-4 into real products. My professional background is 5+ years of remote operations, CRM management, SOP development, and cross-functional coordination.

Based in Lagos. I work without friction across time zones.
